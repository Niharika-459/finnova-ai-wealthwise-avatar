# WealthWise Avatar

### Your Trusted AI Wealth Companion

An AI-powered, privacy-first digital wealth management assistant designed for the **IDBI Innovate Hackathon 2026**.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional wealth management services remain fragmented and inaccessible to many banking customers. Generic investment advice and limited personalization reduce customer engagement and financial growth opportunities.

---

## Our Solution

**WealthWise Avatar** is an AI-powered digital wealth companion integrated into the IDBI Bank mobile application.

The solution analyzes customer-approved financial information to provide personalized, explainable, and goal-based financial guidance through an interactive AI avatar.

Unlike traditional advisory systems, WealthWise Avatar prioritizes **customer consent, privacy, transparency, and trust** while helping users make informed financial decisions.
